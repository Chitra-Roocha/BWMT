My Reflection

Creating a website with HTML and CSS was a difficult but gratifying process. It was thrilling to see my ideas come to life and to have complete control over the website's design and layout.

However, it did need a great deal of time and attention to detail. Understanding the link between HTML and CSS was one of the most difficult things I had to learn. CSS is in charge of styling and appearance, whereas HTML is in charge of structure and content.

It took some effort to figure out how to connect the two and make sure the website looked the way I wanted it to. Despite the difficulties, creating a simple HTML/CSS website was a very satisfying experience.

It gave me a feeling of achievement to see my ideas come to life, and I learned essential skills that I may use to future projects. I'm enthusiastic to keep learning and explore the world of web development.
